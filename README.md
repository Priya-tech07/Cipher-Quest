# 🕵️‍♂️ CipherQuest: Crack the Code

**CipherQuest** is a premium, interactive cryptography training application built with SwiftUI. Step into the shoes of an elite cryptographer, solve historical and modern ciphers, and climb the ranks of the agency.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🧩 Cryptographic Challenges
- **Multiple Cipher Types**: Master the classics including **Atbash**, **Caesar**, and **Vigenere** ciphers.
- **Story Mode**: Embark on a progressive campaign with over 50 increasing difficulty levels.
- **Specialized Categories**: Solve puzzles themed around **Coding**, **Cricket**, **Cinema**, **History**, and **Geography**.
- **Daily Intelligence**: Solve a unique **Daily Challenge** every day and build your streak on the persistence calendar.
- **Practice Missions**: Train on specific cipher types with variable difficulty levels (Easy, Hard, Difficult).

### 👤 Agent Identity
- **Custom Codename**: Register your agent identity during onboarding.
- **Detailed Stats**: Track your mission success, coin balance, and XP.
- **Badges & Achievements**: Earn prestigious badges including:
  - 🛠 **Developer** & 🏗 **System Architect**
  - 🛡 **Cyber Sentinel** & 🔐 **Security Master**
  - 🏆 **Grand Master**
  - 🏏 **Cricket Champion**, 🎬 **Cinema Buff**, 📜 **History Scholar**, and 🌎 **Geography Explorer**
- **Riddle Archive**: Re-visit every secure message you've successfully decrypted.

### 🎓 Field Intel
- **Interactive Onboarding**: A dynamic, geometry-aware tutorial that guides you through the agency's tools.
- **How to Solve Guides**: Detailed field guides for every cipher type to help you when intel is scarce.
- **Adaptive UI**: Seamlessly toggle between **Light** and **Dark** modes with a premium glassmorphic interface.

---

## 🚀 Tech Stack

- **Framework**: SwiftUI
- **Architecture**: MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel)
- **Persistence**: UserDefaults with a custom `UserDefaultsManager` for agent stats and progress.
- **Design**: Premium glassmorphic UI, custom HSL color palettes, and responsive geometry-based animations.
- **Logic**: Scalable Factory Pattern for cipher implementations and puzzle generation.

---

## 🛠 Installation

1. Open the project in **Xcode** or **Swift Playgrounds**.
2. Run on your preferred **iPhone** or **iPad** simulator.
3. The app is fully responsive and optimized for all iOS screen sizes.
